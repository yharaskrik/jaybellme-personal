About

Hi, my name is Jay Bell. I am an entrepreneur and software developer. I have worked on many projects from thermal imaging to mobile apps. Scroll down to learn more!

I graduated Thompson Rivers University from the Computer System: Operations and Management diploma program and am currently finishing my undergraduate in Computer Science at the University of British Columbia Okanagan.

I have been a part of three startups in the past: Sha Software, Hummingbird Drones and Unite. You can read more about them below! I am interested in getting into machine learning, artificial intelligence and neural networks.

I am currently working on any little projects that I can find or think of. In the past I have competed in Two Startup Weekends, in one we won Best Use of Cloud Technology for a crowdfunding platform for third world countries that utilized Bitcoin. While the other we had a little more fun and won Wildest Team. Since then I helped plan another Startup Weekend with the unofficial, but not entirely self given, title of Assistant Deputy Facilitator and will be returning to plan another this year.
